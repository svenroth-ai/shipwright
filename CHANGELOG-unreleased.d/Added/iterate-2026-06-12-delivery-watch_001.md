F11 Delivery-Watch (`shared/scripts/tools/watch_pr_delivery.py`): an iterate is "delivered" only when its PR is actually MERGED with all Required Checks green, confirmed by polling `gh pr view` to a terminal state — arming auto-merge and walking away ("shoot and forget") is no longer treated as done.
