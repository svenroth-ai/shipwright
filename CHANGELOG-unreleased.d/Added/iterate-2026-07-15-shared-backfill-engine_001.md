Shared `backfill_test_links` engine (traceability TT6): maps a repo's existing tests to the FRs they cover — deterministic-first (path/split-prefix, introducing-commit `affected_frs`), LLM-assisted-second (opt-in, advisory-only). Writes high-confidence `@FR` tags into test files (idempotent), lists low-confidence proposals with confidences, and surfaces orphan candidates as `confirmed`/`possible`/`unmapped` — never auto-deleting a test. Offline by default; the opt-in LLM leg sends only paths + titles + FR ids. Shared by adopt (TT7) and the retrofit step (TT8); its tags feed the TT1 traceability manifest.
