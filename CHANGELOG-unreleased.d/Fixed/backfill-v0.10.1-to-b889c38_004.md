**`scripts/update-marketplace.sh` Step 4 file-copy fallback for Windows.** When the plugin mirror at `cache/shipwright/plugins/<plugin>/` was a real directory rather than a symlink (Windows: junction-detection can fail; manual `cp -r` substitution; antivirus-induced symlink rejection), the `if [ -L "$link_path" ]` branch silently skipped — leaving stale plugin caches that diverged from the marketplace until manual cleanup. Now: real-dir branches into a per-file copy fallback that mirrors the symlink-target tree, idempotent across re-runs (commit `2d7a475`).
