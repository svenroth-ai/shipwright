**Adopt crawler made SPA-aware: configurable wait-strategy + settle (`shared/templates/crawler.ts.template`).** Polling-heavy SPAs (TanStack Query, SWR) and lazy-mounted nav defeated the previous "wait for `load` + 500 ms, scrape `<a href>`" approach: BFS saw zero same-origin links on the entry page and exited at depth 0, and screenshots raced with re-renders triggered by 1 s polling. Template now reads `SHIPWRIGHT_CRAWL_WAIT_STRATEGY` (`load` default, `networkidle` / `domcontentloaded` opt-in) and `SHIPWRIGHT_CRAWL_SETTLE_MS` (default 1500 ms, was hardcoded 500); screenshot stability protected by post-network-idle settle pause (commit `2d65401`).
