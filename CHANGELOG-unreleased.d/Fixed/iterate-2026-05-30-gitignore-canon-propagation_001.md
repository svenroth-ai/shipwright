Framework `.shipwright/` gitignore rules now propagate to consuming projects: `shared/templates/shipwright-gitignore.template` is the canonical SSoT, idempotently merged into a project's `.gitignore` by `/shipwright-adopt` and `/shipwright-project` (and self-healable via `gitignore_canon.py`). A drift test keeps the template congruent with the framework's own `.gitignore`, so a future ADR adding a gitignored `.shipwright/` dir auto-propagates everywhere (closes the gap that left shipwright-webui with untracked `runtime/` clutter).
