The requirements catalog no longer claims that every completed change records the requirements it touched. Measured against this project's own change log, a minority do — 61 of 342 — so the previous wording promised a completeness the records do not have. Checking the new query against the change history that was recently moved out of the catalog also found three changes that shipped but were never recorded against the requirement they affected; those remain findable in the commit history and in the planning documents, and the gap is now pinned by a test so it cannot quietly widen or quietly disappear.
