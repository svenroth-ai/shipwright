Control Grade **Test-Health** now reflects diff-coverage: when the changed lines are under-tested (below 80% of changed lines covered) the dimension shows a WARN and a moderate score reduction, so a green-but-untested change no longer reads as full test health. It never hard-caps the grade to F (that stays a real test-suite collapse), and repos that supply no diff-coverage signal are unaffected. (diff-coverage roadmap Phase 3)
